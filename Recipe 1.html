Sugar Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup of butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 375 degrees Fahenheit.
  2. In a small bowl, mix togther the flour, baking soda, and baking powder, then set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the butter and sugar togther until smooth. Beat in the egg and vanilla. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients.
  4. Roll handfulls of dough into balls, and place onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  5. Bake the cookies for 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
  6. Remove cookies from the oven and allow them to cool before eating.
